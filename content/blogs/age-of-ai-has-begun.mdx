> 原文链接：[The Age of AI Has Begun](https://www.gatesnotes.com/The-Age-of-AI-Has-Begun)

这两天我重温 Bill Gate 在 23 年写的一篇文章，里面在当时看起来是“愿景”的已经成为现实（大佬们的远见😭），我摘抄了几个原文精彩片段：

> The development of AI is as fundamental as the creation of the microprocessor, the personal computer, the Internet, and the mobile phone. It will change the way people work, learn, travel, get health care, and communicate with each other. Entire industries will reorient around it. Businesses will distinguish themselves by how well they use it.

**💡 我：** Bill Gates 把 AI 和 GUI 类比，**GUI 时代催生了 Windows / Office ，移动时代催生了 iPhone / 微信 / 支付宝，**那么 AI Native 时代会催生什么？我觉得是**"以 Agent 为中心的工作流工具"** ，目前大部分的工具、软件是都是"人手动 + 工具辅助"，而 AI Native 时代的 工具会反过来：**"Agent 主导 + 人干预"**。

> Eventually your main way of controlling a computer will no longer be pointing and clicking or tapping on menus and dialogue boxes. Instead, you’ll be able to write a request in plain English. (And not just English—AIs will understand languages from around the world.)

**💡 我：** 这不就是妥妥的 prompt 吗

> In addition, advances in AI will enable the creation of a personal agent. Think of it as a digital personal assistant: It will see your latest emails, know about the meetings you attend, read what you read, and read the things you don’t want to bother with. This will both improve your work on the tasks you want to do and free you from the ones you don’t want to do.

> Company-wide agents will empower employees in new ways. An agent that understands a particular company will be available for its employees to consult directly and should be part of every meeting so it can answer questions. It can be told to be passive or encouraged to speak up if it has some insight. It will need access to the sales, support, finance, product schedules, and text related to the company. It should read news related to the industry the company is in. 

**💡 我：** 前段时间的 Agent 可以阅读飞书文档，可以直接回复评论，现在 Agent 已经可以入会了，**身临其境感受到 Agent 在会议上开口说话，漫威电影里面 Javis 的即视感**

最后，还有几个感受：

- 2026 年的今天，我们不再讨论 AI 会不会发生，而是讨论**以多快的速度、以什么形态、在哪些行业先完成重构（好了，保住工作好运符🐶🐶🐶）**
- 对产品经理的提醒是：不要只盯着“AI 能做什么新功能”，而要问——**如果从今天开始重新设计这个产品，交互、流程、商业模式、组织协作会怎么变？**
- 模型不断进化，**真正形成壁垒的是场景理解、工作流沉淀、数据闭环和用户信任**
- AI 浪潮里最先吃到红利的，不是“最懂模型的人”，而是**最懂用户痛点、又能把模型能力稳妥接进真实工作流的人**
