## Project Overview

**Tab Clean** is more than just a "tab closer." It is a workspace management system designed for power users who treat browser tabs as temporary tasks, research context, and "unfinished intentions."

**Core Value:** Transforming high-entropy tab accumulation into a structured task flow.

## 01 Problem

Knowledge workers often leave dozens or hundreds of tabs open not because they are "lazy," but because they are **afraid to close them**. Every tab represents a potential resource, a reminder of a task, or a thread of a research project.

## 02 Insight: Tabs are Unfinished Intentions

My core insight during research was that **Tabs are ephemeral states of work.** Most tabs are opened with the intention of being processed "later." When "later" doesn't happen, they become mental debt.

## 03 Strategy: A Decision System, Not Just a Cleaner

Instead of overwhelming the user with "Smart AI Summaries" or "Complex Folder Systems," Tab Clean V1 focuses on **Decision Support**. It answers one question: **"What should I do with this tab right now?"**

## 04 Experience Design: Why Side Panel?

I chose the **Chrome Side Panel** over a traditional Popup for three strategic reasons:

- **Side-by-Side Context:** Users can view their current page while managing their workspace.
- **Space for Complexity:** Organizing 100+ items requires the vertical space and structure that a small popup cannot provide.
- **Workflow Integration:** It feels like a "Native Tool" rather than an "Add-on," encouraging daily habit formation.

## 05 PRD Notes & Original Thinking (中文)

**核心洞察：Tab 即待办**

用户习惯把浏览器 Tab 当成“待办列表”，但 Tab 缺乏状态管理。Tab Clean 的核心价值不在于“关掉”，而在于“归类状态”。

- **状态互斥原则：** 重复项专区优先级最高，同一时刻一个 Tab 只出现在一个区域，确保用户决策路径不交叉。
- **一键瘦身逻辑：** 重复 Tab 默认保留“最近访问”的一个，其余批量清理，这种“最近最少使用（LRU）”的思想降低了用户的选择焦虑。
- **情感化加载：** 设计了陪伴式加载动画，缓解用户在面对 100+ 混乱 Tab 时的挫败感，变“清理垃圾”为“整理工作台”。
