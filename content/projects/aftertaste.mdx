## Project Overview

**Aftertaste** is an AI-powered personal life-logging and behavioral awareness tool. It is designed to help users transition from "mindless" consumption to "mindful" awareness. By integrating minimal AI-assisted interactions, the tool transforms routine spending into a structured reflective process, helping users regain control over their daily habits and emotional well-being.

## 01 Problem

For many urban professionals, consumption—buying coffee, milk tea, or snacks—has become a "mindless habit." These small, frequent expenses often serve as a default coping mechanism for stress, but they rarely leave a lasting positive impression. The core issue isn't just financial; it's the **loss of awareness** during the consumption moment.

## 02 Insight: Consumption as a Window into Self-Awareness

My core insight is that consumption acts as a perfect data point for observing one’s mental state. Instead of focusing solely on the "cost" (which leads to guilt), I pivoted the focus to "value." If we can provide a thoughtful, empathetic response at the exact moment of purchase, we can transform a mechanical habit into an opportunity for self-reflection.

## 03. Strategy

Instead of building a traditional bookkeeping app, I designed Aftertaste as a **Cognitive Feedback System**. It answers one question: "How does this purchase resonate with my current state of mind?" It prioritizes psychological well-being over raw financial metrics.

## 04. Experience Design

Support voice, photo, and text input, capturing a moment should take less than 5 seconds. Moving away from complex forms makes logging feel like a mindful pause, not an administrative chore.

## 05. PRD Notes & Original Thinking

**核心洞察：消费即觉知**  
Aftertaste 的价值在于提供一个“认知缓冲期”，通过 AI 有温度的反馈，将“习惯性下单”转变为“觉知记录”。

**肯定+反思原则**  
反馈逻辑必须遵循先共情（承认当下的情绪，如疲惫、社交需要）再引导思考（是否有更好的替代方案）。这种温和的规训比冷冰冰的记账提醒更易于被用户接纳。

**拿铁因子可视化**  
设计了“觉知余温 (Awareness Temperature)”仪表盘，将抽象的情绪与消费指标转化为直观的温度指标，让用户直观看到自己的生活节奏与心态波动。
