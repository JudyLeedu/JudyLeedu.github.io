## Project Overview
**AI News Intelligence Bot** monitors scattered AI updates across newsletters, RSS feeds, and web sources, then turns them into structured insight briefs delivered through Lark.

**Core Value:** Turning fragmented AI news into a structured intelligence workflow that helps busy product thinkers separate signal from noise, build context over time, and make faster sense of market and technology shifts.

## 01 Problem
AI news moves faster than most teams can process. New model releases, product launches, funding updates, regulation changes, and agent workflows appear every day. The problem is no longer whether information exists; the problem is whether a busy PM can identify what deserves attention.

## 02 Insight: AI News Is Not Content, It Is a Decision Input
News is valuable only when it helps answer a decision question: what changed, why does it matter, and whether it affects product direction.

## 03. Strategy: A Decision System, Not Just a Cleaner
Instead of building a single push notification tool, I designed the product around three different reading scenarios. Each frequency solves a different user job.

- **Daily Expresso:** A fast daily scan for awareness. The goal is to help users catch important AI updates in a few minutes without opening multiple sources.
- **Weekly Brew:** A deeper weekly read for context-building. The goal is to connect individual news items into themes, patterns, and product implications.
- **Monthly Hand-Drip:** A monthly reflection layer for long-term understanding. The goal is to convert fast-moving updates into durable industry memory.

## 04. Original Thinking 
**核心洞察：资讯不是越多越好，而是要在正确的时间给到正确粒度。**
日报解决“今天有什么变化”，周报解决“这些变化意味着什么”，月报解决“这些变化是否形成趋势”。这三个问题不同，所以不应该用同一种推送形态回答。

## 05. Next Steps
- **Knowledge Base Integration:** Save high-value weekly and monthly insights into a persistent Lark knowledge base for long-term retrieval.
- **Multi-modal News Understanding:** Extend the pipeline from text-only sources to product screenshots, launch videos, and visual announcements.
